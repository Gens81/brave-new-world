Brave New Hell is a set of difficulty patches made for Brave New World veterans seeking a challenge.

It's composed by:
- 2x enemy speed
Original hack by Nowea that doubles the enemy speed.
- Nerfed elemental weakness
Exploiting elemental weakness deals 1.5x damage in place of 2x.
- Nerfed HP/MP ELs
HP and MP Esper Levels are simplified to be +20 if paired with another stat, +40 if alone.
- Levels cap 30/20
Max Level is 30, max ELs are 20. These are the typical endgame values.
- Item cap 20
Can't borrow more than 20 for each item in the inventory.

Single patches composing it are available to download to customize the experience.
